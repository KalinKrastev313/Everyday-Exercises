The script is one possible solution of the following exercise: 

2. Drink Something
Kids drink toddy, Teens drink coke, Young adults drink beer, 
Adults drink whisky.
Make a program that receives an age, and returns what they drink.
Rules:
Kids under 14 years old.
Teens under 18 years old.
Young adults under 21 years old.
Adults above 21.
Note: All the values except the last one are inclusive!

Input 		Output
13 		drink toddy
17 		drink coke
21 		drink beer
30 		drink whisky
